Who is J. Cole?

J. Cole is an MC and producer who has achieved phenomenal success over the last few years, a result of the long hours he spent grinding hard on the mixtape scene and sharing songs on internet forums.

Since signing to Jay Z's Roc Nation, he has toured worldwide, sold huge amounts of records, produced for Kendrick Lamar and even come to the attention of Barack Obama.

Along with establishing his own nonprofit foundation, he keeps up a busy release schedule.
